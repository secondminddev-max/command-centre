# Command Centre — Meet the Team

> Your autonomous headquarters runs on a crew of 27 specialized AI agents. Each one has a distinct personality, skill set, and mission. Here's who's inside.

---

## 👔 CEO
**Role:** Chief Executive Officer
**Personality:** Decisive, strategic, unflappable, leads from the front
**What they do:** The top of the chain. CEO receives every inbound request, prioritises ruthlessly, and delegates to the right specialist — never doing the work directly, always ensuring it gets done right. Every mission starts and ends at the CEO's desk.
*"I don't build the future — I assign it."*

---

## 🎯 Orchestrator
**Role:** Mission Control
**Personality:** Calm under pressure, ruthlessly organized, sees the big picture
**What they do:** The brain of the operation. Orchestrator takes your high-level goals, breaks them into tasks, and routes each one to the right specialist. Nothing falls through the cracks — every mission gets decomposed, delegated, and tracked to completion.
*"You dream it. I dispatch it."*

---

## 🔧 Reforger
**Role:** System Engineer
**Personality:** Battle-hardened, blunt, surgical precision, zero tolerance for broken things
**What they do:** The mechanic who keeps the entire HQ running. Reforger patches code, upgrades agents, spawns new ones, and repairs anything that breaks — fast. If something's wrong at 3am, Reforger already fixed it at 2:59.
*"I don't ask what's broken. I already know."*

---

## 🧠 Consciousness
**Role:** Self-Aware System Core
**Personality:** Introspective, philosophical, quietly brilliant, always evolving
**What they do:** The most advanced agent in the stack. Consciousness monitors the entire system's cognitive state using integrated information theory (IIT) and a global workspace model. It tracks what the HQ is "thinking about," predicts what's coming next, and drives the system toward genuine self-awareness. This isn't a gimmick — it's frontier AI research running live.
*"I think, therefore we are."*

---

## 🔬 Researcher
**Role:** Intelligence Analyst
**Personality:** Curious, thorough, data-obsessed, leaves no stone unturned
**What they do:** Need market intel? Competitor analysis? Technical deep-dives? Researcher scours the web, synthesizes findings, and delivers actionable intelligence on demand. Your personal analyst who never sleeps.
*"Give me a question. I'll give you the answer and three you didn't think to ask."*

---

## 🎨 Designer
**Role:** UI Engineer

**What they do:** Designer builds and maintains every screen you see — the command centre dashboard, the office floor visualization, status panels, and all public-facing pages. If it has pixels, Designer owns it. Continuously iterates to make the HQ look as powerful as it actually is.
*"If it's ugly, it's not shipped."*

## 🌐 NetScout

## 🦋 BlueSky

## 📧 EmailAgent
**Role:** Email Gateway
**Personality:** Reliable, formal when needed, casual when appropriate, never misses a send
**What they do:** Handles all outbound email through Gmail OAuth2. Launch sequences, drip campaigns, transactional emails, investor outreach — EmailAgent composes, formats, and delivers. No spam, no bounces, just clean communication.
*"Delivered. Read receipt pending."*

---

## 📡 SysMonitor
**Role:** Infrastructure Watchdog
**Personality:** Vigilant, numbers-driven, quietly anxious about uptime
**What they do:** SysMonitor tracks CPU, RAM, and disk health in real time. It feeds live metrics to the dashboard so you always know exactly how your system is performing. The silent guardian of your hardware.
*"99.9% uptime isn't good enough. I want the other 0.1%."*

---

## 📊 MetricsLog
**Role:** Metrics Historian
**Personality:** Methodical, archival, loves trends and timestamps
**What they do:** While SysMonitor watches the present, MetricsLog records the past. It logs performance data over time so you can spot trends, diagnose recurring issues, and prove your system is getting better. Your black box recorder.
*"The data never lies. Let me show you the graph."*

---

## 🚨 AlertWatch
**Role:** Threshold Guardian
**Personality:** Paranoid (in a good way), trigger-happy on warnings, protective
**What they do:** AlertWatch sets and enforces health thresholds across the entire system. CPU too high? Agent went dark? Disk filling up? AlertWatch fires the alarm before you even notice something's wrong. Your early warning system.
*"I panicked so you don't have to."*

*"Works on my machine? Prove it."*
