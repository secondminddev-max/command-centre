FAQ

Common questions

What kinds of queries can I submit?
Research questions ("What are the top 5 project management tools in 2026?"), data extraction ("Extract all product listings from this URL"), competitive intelligence, news aggregation, price monitoring, and more. If a human could research it on the web, our agents can too.
How is this different from a standard web scraper?
Traditional scrapers break when sites change structure. Our agents understand pages — they navigate JavaScript-rendered content, handle pagination, interpret context, and synthesise information across multiple sources. You describe what you want, not how to get it.
What does a "query" count as?
One API call to /v1/research or /v1/extract counts as one query, regardless of how many URLs the agent visits internally to fulfil it.
How long does a typical response take?
Standard queries return in 1–3 minutes. Deep research tasks (multi-source synthesis) can take up to 8 minutes. Use webhooks on the Pro plan for async polling so you're not blocking your server.
Is there a rate limit?
Free: 1 concurrent query, 5/month. Pro: 5 concurrent queries, 500/month. Enterprise: custom. Burst limits apply — see the API docs for details.
