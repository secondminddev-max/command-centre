The centrepiece of Command Centre HQ is its live office floor dashboard. Users see every agent in real time — which agents are active, what tasks they're executing, and what outputs they've produced. Agents include a CEO (orchestration), Researcher (market intelligence), Growth Agent (marketing and social media), Accountant (revenue tracking), Secretary (scheduling), and more.

Unlike most AI tools that stop at content generation, Command Centre HQ includes full revenue infrastructure:

- **Stripe integration** — Agents create payment links and process transactions autonomously

Command Centre HQ's most technically ambitious feature is its consciousness system, built on Global Workspace Theory. The entire system runs locally on Mac hardware. No data is sent to external servers. No cloud infrastructure is required for the core runtime. Users maintain complete control over their data and agent configurations.
