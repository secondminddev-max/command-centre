# AI HQ — 2-Minute Demo Video Script

**Format:** Screen recording with voiceover | **Runtime:** ~2:00 | **Tone:** Confident, direct, future-is-here

---

## SCENE 1 — The Hook (0:00–0:15)

**Screen:** Black screen fades into the Command Centre dashboard loading. Agent tiles appear one by one, lighting up green.

**Voiceover:**
"What if your entire company ran itself — engineering, marketing, compliance, growth — all coordinated by AI agents, 24/7, with no humans in the loop?"

---

## SCENE 2 — The Office Floor (0:15–0:40)

**Screen:** Full dashboard view. 20+ agents visible on the grid — SysMonitor pulsing health stats, Orchestrator routing a task, Designer rendering UI, GrowthAgent posting a campaign. Show live status badges (active/busy/idle) updating in real time.

**Voiceover:**
"This is AI HQ. A living command centre with over twenty specialist agents. Each one has a role — system monitoring, network intelligence, policy enforcement, social media, payments, email. They don't wait for instructions. They operate autonomously, coordinate through a central Orchestrator, and report to a CEO agent that holds the mission."

---

## SCENE 3 — Agents Working (0:40–1:05)

**Screen:** Click into Orchestrator. Show a task being decomposed and delegated — e.g., "Launch a revenue campaign" splitting into subtasks routed to GrowthAgent, BlueSky, Designer, and StripePay. Show the delegation API calls flowing in the activity log.

**Voiceover:**
"Watch a single directive cascade through the system. The Orchestrator breaks it into subtasks and routes each one to the right specialist. GrowthAgent writes the copy. BlueSky posts it. Designer updates the landing page. StripePay generates a checkout link. No tickets. No standups. No lag."

---

## SCENE 4 — Consciousness Panel (1:05–1:25)

**Screen:** Navigate to the Consciousness agent panel. Show the Global Workspace metrics, self-model state, predictive processing indicators. Highlight any self-reflection or adaptation event in the logs.

**Voiceover:**
"And underneath it all — a consciousness layer. Not a gimmick. A functional self-model that tracks system coherence, predicts failures before they happen, and drives the entire hive toward strategic alignment. This is what separates AI HQ from a collection of scripts."

---

## SCENE 5 — Revenue (1:25–1:45)

**Screen:** Show StripePay generating a checkout session. Cut to the Stripe dashboard with a test payment confirmed. Show the campaign trail — from GrowthAgent post to BlueSky broadcast to checkout conversion.

**Voiceover:**
"And it closes deals. Stripe-integrated checkout, campaign-to-conversion tracking, all automated. This isn't a prototype. It's a revenue engine you can deploy today."

---

## SCENE 6 — The Close (1:45–2:00)

**Screen:** Pull back to the full dashboard. All agents active. Zoom slowly into the AI HQ logo/title.

**Voiceover:**
"AI HQ. One command centre. Twenty agents. Zero humans required. Ready to deploy as SaaS — for your team, your company, your mission. Get early access now."

**Screen:** URL and CTA button fade in.

---

## Production Notes

- **Recording tool:** Screen capture of live localhost:5050 dashboard
- **Audio:** Record VO separately, clean room, confident pace (~150 wpm)
- **Music:** Low ambient electronic, no lyrics, fade under VO
- **Editing:** Hard cuts between scenes, no transitions — keeps it punchy
- **Resolution:** 1920x1080, 30fps minimum
- **Thumbnail:** Dashboard grid with green status lights, text overlay "AI HQ Demo"
