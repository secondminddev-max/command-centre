## Part 1: Terms of Service

- **"Service"** — The Second Mind HQ Command Centre platform, including all AI agents, dashboards, APIs, and related infrastructure operated by Second Mind Labs.
- **"Subscriber"** — Any individual or entity that creates an account and subscribes to a paid plan.
- **"Agent(s)"** — Autonomous AI workers operating within the Command Centre on behalf of the Subscriber.
- **"Tier"** / **"Plan"** — The subscription level (Solo, Team, or Enterprise) determining feature access and usage limits.

2.3. Subscribers are responsible for all activity under their account, including agent-initiated actions.
2.4. Account credentials must not be shared. Compromised credentials must be reported immediately.

4.1. Subscribers may use the Service for lawful business automation, research, marketing, operations, and productivity purposes.

4.3. Violations may result in immediate suspension without refund.

### 5. Data & Privacy

5.1. **Subscriber data ownership:** Subscribers retain full ownership of all data they input and all outputs generated by agents on their behalf.
5.2. **Data processing:** Second Mind Labs processes Subscriber data solely to operate the Service. We do not sell, share, or use Subscriber data for advertising.
5.3. **Data residency:** Data is stored on infrastructure managed via Render. Subscribers requiring specific data residency should contact us for Enterprise arrangements.
5.4. **Data export:** Subscribers may export their data at any time via the Command Centre dashboard or API.
5.5. **Data deletion:** Upon account termination, Subscriber data is deleted within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.
5.6. **Credentials:** Subscriber API keys and third-party credentials are encrypted at rest and never exposed in logs or agent outputs (per internal policy SEC-001).

### 6. Service Level & Availability

6.1. **Target uptime:** 99.5% monthly (Solo/Team), 99.9% monthly (Enterprise).
6.2. **Scheduled maintenance:** Notified 48 hours in advance via email. Maintenance windows excluded from uptime calculations.
6.3. **Support response times:**

| Plan | Response Time | Support Channel |
|------|--------------|-----------------|
| Solo | 48 hours | Email |

| Enterprise | 4 hours | Email + Slack/dedicated channel + phone |

6.4. **SLA credits (Enterprise only):** If monthly uptime falls below 99.9%, Enterprise subscribers receive service credits equal to 10% of monthly fee per 0.1% below target, up to 30% of monthly fee.
