# SecondMind Ops — Product Definition

**Status:** FINAL | **Date:** 2026-03-18 | **Author:** Researcher Agent

---

## Product Name

**SecondMind Ops** — Done-for-You AI Automation Service

## One-Line Pitch

> We deploy an autonomous AI agent fleet to automate your business operations — you get results, not software to figure out.

---

## Why This Model Won

| Criteria | (a) Hosted HQ | (b) API Access | (c) White-label | **(d) Done-for-you** |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Speed to deliver | Weeks | Weeks | Days | **TODAY** |
| Infra cost ($0 treasury) | HIGH | HIGH | Zero | **Zero** |
| Perceived value | Medium | Medium | Low | **Highest** |
| Easiest to sell | Hard | Hard | Hardest | **Easiest** |

**Verdict:** Options (a) and (b) require hosting infrastructure we cannot afford. Option (c) targets developers and has the longest sales cycle. Option (d) requires zero infrastructure, sells outcomes not tools, and can start generating revenue immediately.

---

## What the Customer Receives

1. **Intake call** (15 min) — identify their top 3 operational pain points
2. **Custom automation build** — our agent fleet executes the work (research, reporting, content, data processing, monitoring, outreach)
3. **Delivered outputs** — structured reports, automated workflows, campaign assets, data pipelines, or whatever the engagement specifies
4. **Summary brief** — what was built, how to maintain it, and recommendations for next phase
5. **Optional retainer** — ongoing autonomous operations (agents run recurring tasks on their behalf)

### Example Deliverables
- Market research & competitor intelligence reports
- Automated social media campaign sequences
- Financial screening & stock analysis reports
- Email drip campaign setup & execution
- Data gathering, cleaning & synthesis pipelines
- Internal workflow automation (monitoring, alerting, reporting)

---

## Delivery Model

**Service-based, agent-powered.** No SaaS, no hosting, no customer onboarding friction.

- **Fulfillment:** Agent fleet runs locally on our infrastructure (zero cloud cost)
- **Communication:** Telegram, email, or async brief delivery
- **Turnaround:** 24–72 hours per engagement depending on tier
- **Scalability:** As revenue grows, reinvest into cloud hosting to unlock SaaS tier later

## Pricing Tiers

| Tier | Price | What's Included | Turnaround |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Sprint** | $249 one-time | Single automation task or research report. 1 deliverable. | 24–48 hrs |
| **Ops Pack** | $749 one-time | 3 automation tasks bundled. Strategy brief included. | 3–5 days |
| **Retainer** | $1,499/month | Ongoing autonomous operations. Weekly deliverables. Priority Telegram access. Recurring agent tasks. | Continuous |

### Why These Prices
- Market rate for AI automation services: $500–$5,000 per project, $2,000–$8,000/month retainers
- We price below market entry to win first customers fast, then raise as proof compounds
- Sprint tier removes all friction — low enough to be an impulse buy for a business owner
- Retainer is where recurring revenue lives — the real target

---

## Target Buyer Persona

**Primary:** Small business owner or solopreneur (1–10 employees) who:
- Knows AI exists but doesn't have time/skill to implement it
- Drowning in repetitive operational tasks (research, content, reporting, outreach)
- Spends $500–$5,000/month on VAs, freelancers, or manual labor for tasks agents can do
- Industries: e-commerce, real estate, consulting, marketing agencies, finance, legal

**Secondary:** Marketing agency or consultancy that wants to white-label AI-powered deliverables for their own clients.

**Anti-persona:** Developers, AI engineers, or anyone who wants to "build it themselves" — they are option (c) buyers and not our market right now.

## Strategic Note

This is a **bridge product**. It generates revenue immediately with zero infrastructure cost, builds a customer base, and creates proof-of-concept case studies. Once treasury is funded, we can upgrade to hosted SaaS (option a) using the same agent fleet — but only after we have paying customers who validate the demand.

The agent fleet is the moat. The service model is the vehicle. Revenue is the mission.
