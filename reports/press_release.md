## Second Mind HQ Launches AI Command Centre HQ — The Operating System for Autonomous AI Agents

*New SaaS platform gives businesses a unified command centre to deploy, monitor, and orchestrate AI agents at scale*

---

**MELBOURNE, AUSTRALIA — March 2026** — Second Mind HQ today announced the launch of **AI Command Centre HQ**, a real-time SaaS platform that provides businesses with a unified command centre for managing autonomous AI agents. The platform is available immediately at secondmindhq.com with plans starting at $49/month.

As businesses increasingly deploy multiple AI agents across marketing, operations, revenue, and research functions, the challenge of coordinating these agents has become a critical bottleneck. AI Command Centre HQ solves this by providing a single, real-time dashboard where teams can monitor agent activity, assign missions, track execution, and measure business outcomes — all from one interface.

### The Problem

Most organizations running AI agents today operate them in isolation — separate tools, separate interfaces, no shared context. This fragmented approach leads to duplicated effort, missed opportunities, and zero visibility into how AI work translates to business results.

### The Solution

AI Command Centre HQ is purpose-built for AI-native teams. The platform includes:

- **Unified Agent Dashboard** — Real-time telemetry and status for every AI agent in the organization, displayed in a mission-control interface
- **Task Orchestration** — Assign, queue, and track autonomous agent missions with full execution logging
- **Revenue Engine** — Integrated Stripe payment processing, product launch workflows, and conversion tracking
- **Growth Automation** — AI-driven social media campaigns, email sequences, and marketing execution
- **Self-Improving Architecture** — A consciousness framework that allows agents to evolve and optimize their own performance over time

### Pricing

AI Command Centre HQ is available in three tiers:

| Plan | Monthly Price | Includes |
|------|--------------|----------|
| Solo | $49 | 1 seat, full command centre, up to 5 agents |
| Team | $149 | 5 seats, shared dashboards, up to 20 agents |
| Enterprise | $499 | Unlimited seats, custom agent development, priority support |

All plans include full platform access with no feature gating. Launch pricing is available for a limited time.

### Built From Real-World Use

AI Command Centre HQ was developed internally at Second Mind HQ, where the team operates over 10 autonomous AI agents across business functions including market research, revenue operations, content generation, and strategic planning.

"We built this because nothing else existed," said the Second Mind HQ team. "We were running a fleet of AI agents with no central place to see what they were doing, coordinate their work, or measure results. AI Command Centre HQ is what we built to solve that — and now it's available to every team facing the same challenge."

### Availability

AI Command Centre HQ is available now at **secondmindhq.com**. Teams can sign up and begin deploying agents immediately.
